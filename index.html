OCRR VISION

Vision


To help the underprivileged communities or underdeveloped societies to come out of the vicious cycle of poverty and be able to achieve what they deserve in the area of 21st centuries.

Goal


To implement various humanitarian projects in the fields of Health Care Services, education, vocational training, introduction of modern agricultural and Farming technology and conduction of research and the usage of its result for further improvements.

Mission


To work shoulder by shoulder with all communities to reduce continues suffering, provide better health and educational facilities, increase the chances of their work force marketability and eventually enhance their standard of living.

Our Purpose

Organization for community Rehabilitation and Relief A team of benevolent youths with aim to bring positive impact in society.

Health services delivery and improvement

Provision of a high standard, impartial and sustainable health services all over Afghanistan, particularly to the most underserved communities of the rural areas.

Education and vocational trainings

Improvement of educational system and facilities Provision of the educational facilities in the un covered areas, especially for the children of poor families, who are without any financial mean or support.

Agriculture, animals rearing and rural areas Rehabilitation and development

Introduction and distribution of high quality seeds (through practical farming in small pieces of lands) Rehabilitation of destroyed irrigation system and equipping them with new technology Establishment and support of animals’ farming.

Health services delivery and improvement

Capacity building of the health workers, through conduction of short or long term educational courses, seminars and workshops.

Education and vocational trainings

Provision of educational facilities for children and adults who has fallen back from the formal education system, especially for women Provision of vocational training for youths, who has completed the school, but could not get into higher education system ladder.

Health services delivery and improvement

Encouragement of the community involvement in their health service delivery by increasing of awareness and sense of ownership.

Agriculture, animals rearing and rural areas Rehabilitation and development

Introduction and distribution of new pesticides Encouragement of the farmers for the eradication of poppy fields and introduction of replacement plants or seeds Participating in the Rural community’s development and cohesion programs.

Health services delivery and improvement

Involvement in the control or relief of natural and human made disasters Promotion of daily hygiene and provision of clean drinking water.

Education and vocational trainings

Capacity building of national staff in their professional fields through coordination with national and international academic institutes or universities.
